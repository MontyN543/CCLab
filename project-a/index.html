Ozirith

Scientific Name:

Radiosonus auraflux

Discovered by / at:

Discovered bya gorup of scientist in an underground military lab

Family:

Electrophporidae- a class of spectral, radio-based entities that absorb and transmit energy waves.

Habitat:

Abandoned laboratories, Area 51

Appearance:

Has a decieving ordinary retro radio body, sitting still until you try to move the knob or change it's frequency.

Key Characteristics:

  • Emits sound waves that manifest as visible distortion in the air.
  • Antenna twitches when it gets angry.
  • The main body also shapes violently when aggravated.
  • The antenna can also move on it's own sometimes.

What we know so far about Ozirith? Ozirith came to be from a wartime transmission that was never received. Stuck in an endless loop, it searches for a listener to complete its purpose. It reacts strangely to old frequencies, replaying fragments of unknown voices, as if hoping someone will finally respond.
